Nine of Pentacles

At first glance, this skyclad woman appears quite isolated. Exposed and far from home, she stands amidst the foliage with just a red-tailed hawk for company. But this predator stands for keen intelligence and a fierceness of spirit. They commune together in the sparkling greenery, masters of their realm.

The Nine of Pentacles is its own reward. After sacrificing and devoting yourself to a single purpose, your dedication has paid off. You're now well established in whatever it is that you set out to do, and you can afford the luxuries that come with a life of success. Enjoy it. There will come a time when you are tested and tried. Retain your sense of propriety and discipline, and you will be well prepared when this time befalls you.
