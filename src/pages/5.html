The Hierophant

The Hierophant stands among the stars, clutching scrolls and holding his hand in a sign of benediction. He stands for enduring tradition: the cultures and beliefs that have hewn history into shape. He invites us to conform. To immerse ourselves in a long-standing, man-made system, be it through education, employment, or religion. To play a part in his machine, we must sacrifice some modicum of personal freedom on his altar. Is it worth it? Only you can decide.

The Hierophant is like a constellation - imaginary lines drawn around the chaos of the cosmos that imbue the night sky with meaning.
