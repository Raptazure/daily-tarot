Four of Swords

A boy kneels in snow, shielded from the elements by two snow-laden pines. Their presence muffles all sound, and as a mist billows up before him, he is shrouded in the cold, heavy silence of winter. He rests his hand on the winged Sword, a tangible conduit of the axis mundi. Three silhouetted rapiers lie discarded at his side, erstwhile reminders of industry and power.

The stillness of winter is the perfect environment for quiet, contemplative repose. Take this time to restore and reflect. Amass your strength and gain perspective and clarity. Lay your struggles aside for now, in this moment of peace.
