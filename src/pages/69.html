Six of Pentacles

At the end of the wall, a boy with five Pentacles willingly parts with one of his shiny coins. He hands it to a girl who has nothing, whose face lights up with this sudden turn of events. He bows his head respectfully, saving her the embarrassing scrutiny Of receiving alms. His small sacrifice has breathed fresh life into her finances, and she sees before he: new opportunities and a richer enjoyment of life. She is elated.

Life's imbalance is its greatest strength. To the man that masters the back and forth motion, the perpetual movement propels him into a steady, productive rhythm. Keep it all together by not paying too much attention to one thing. Accept that everything has its time and its place, and it is up to you to decide when it's time to move onwards.
