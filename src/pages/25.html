Four of Wands

Four wands stand together on the edge of the river, linked by a strand of wildflowers. Supporting each other equally, they simply revel in the scintillating joy of existence. The tendrils of incipient energy dance within the secure structure, finding a happy home in the balance and stability.

This card is the first milestone in the journey of the Wands. Everything is coming together nicely, and while things are supporting themselves, take this time to enjoy the success of what you have wrought so far. This is a time of great peace and contentment. It may include family, or a landmark in your life celebrated by ceremony. At the very core it is happinessone of those rare times when life is going your way, and you're aware enough to appreciate it.
