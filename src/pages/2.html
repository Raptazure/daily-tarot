The High Priestess

The High Priestess is the ruler of the subconscious, where ideas are first conceived. Much like Persephone, she treads the surface of the underworld, brimming with insight but biding her time. When the moment is right, she will burst into the sunshine with all she has learned below. But for now, her footsteps ripple the water in the light of the waning moon, which calls for a quiet retreat of imagination and inner reflection.

Counterbalancing the raw power of the Magician, the High Priestess is the internal, introspective side of creation. She is the fertile subconscious where ideas are first brought to life.
