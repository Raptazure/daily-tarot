Page of Chalices

The Page of Chalices offers up his humble gift: a bowl of water. It attracts the rippling river spirit, incandescent fluidity imbibing the essence of emotion. Slaking his thirst at the Page's bowl, it becomes his unwitting companion. They regard each other with newfound candor and complicity, ready to share in an adventure.

Something as simple and pure as a bowl of water can initiate a journey of the heart. Don't hoard your emotions, for fear of exhausting them. Take what little you have and give freely. You will find that whatever you expend will be returned to you in some grander, more compelling form. Surround yourself with love and light. Embark.
