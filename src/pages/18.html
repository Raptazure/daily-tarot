The Moon

The Moon rises full over the river, dazzling the shallows in its brilliant, silver light. A pale reflection of the Sun, it pervades a ghostly realm where the subtle characteristics of objects are thrown into sharp relief. On either side of the river, wolves howl their keening song, crying to the illusionary nature of their goddess. An empty boat rocks in the supple current, sole witness to the dreamlike, emotive scene.
