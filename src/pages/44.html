Nine of Chalices

As the mist rolls away, a man's heart opens to the Sun. His juggled Chalices are suspended in a single burnished arc over his head. He has reached perfect equilibrium, and he knows it too, reveling in the beauty all around him. Blissful, serene, his purposes fulfilled, he settles back into his seat to watch the sunset.

The Nine of Chalices is all about satisfaction. Some ongoing cycle is nearing its completion, and you are about to be fulfilled and at peace. Like a sunset, this will not last forever, but its evanescent beauty makes it all the more precious. Treasure this moment, for its memory will bring you warmth on grey, rainy days.
