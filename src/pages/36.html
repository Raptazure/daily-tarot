Ace of Chalices

A glimmering fountain draws water from the deep and thrusts it upward. As it courses down each echelon, incandescent dew gently mists around it, falling softly back into the river. Autumn leaves fall as the moon, goddess of emotion and intuition, rises full in the background.

When the Ace of Chalices presents itself, some deep 'wellspring of emotional force is bubbling up inside you or around you. It promises the beginnings of an intimate connection, guided by the heart. Open yourself to love and compassion. Draw deep feelings to the surface and let them shine.
