Two of Chalices

The two lovers bathe in the ripples of the burbling Ace. They have revealed all: letting down their barriers and simply rejoicing in unguarded, human connection. They clink glasses and smile; he with a grateful delight and she with a quiet, receptive contentment. Acknowledging the life within each other, they revel in beauty of their love.

By mixing freely with each other, sharing of ourselves without reservation, we allow people to see our true nature. Let your inner beauty course out, inspiring others to do the same. Be welcoming, receptive, and genuinely grateful for others' presence in your life. They will meld and merge with your aura to create something vivacious and free.
