Page of Wands

A vent bursts open on the riverbank and a pillar of flame rushes out. One fiery tendril loops crazily around the blazing column, finally unfurling to capture the Page of Wands. Cast up into the air, he is drawn inward toward the ethereal fire. A nubile conduit, he courses with the energy of the inferno. A power kindles in his spirit and he feels the call to begin something magical.

When you see the Page of Wands in a reading, you are about to set out on a journey of the mind. You have been struck with some novel or wonderful idea, and you have the naive, unfettered enthusiasm of a child to catalyze it. Take risks like you've never been hurt. Believe wholeheartedly in something. Your divine vibrancy will bring your vision to life. Your courage and passion will see it to completion.
